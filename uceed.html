The Exam

What is UCEED?

UCEED (Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design) is a national-level exam conducted by IIT Bombay — the gateway to B.Des programs at IITs and IIITDM Jabalpur.

It evaluates aptitude, visualization, creativity, and design thinking. A B.Des from an IIT opens careers in product design, UX/UI, graphic design, animation, and industrial design.

Our institute focuses on IIT design entrance coaching in Pune with strong creative training, personal attention, and structured exam preparation — for students in 11th, 12th, or their drop year.
