The Programme

What is NID B.Des?

The NID Bachelor of Design (B.Des) is India's most prestigious 4-year undergraduate design programme, offered by the National Institute of Design, an Institute of National Importance under the Government of India. It trains students across disciplines like Product Design, Graphic Design, Animation, Textile Design, and Communication Design. Any student who has completed or is appearing for 12th (any stream) is eligible. My Design Academia offers specialised NID B.Des coaching in Pune from its centre in Viman Nagar, with NID alumni faculty, small batches, and a studio-focused curriculum built around what NID actually tests.

How to Crack NID DAT?

The NID DAT is a two-stage exam: Prelims (MCQ + sketching) and Mains (studio test). Cracking it takes the right guidance, not just hard work. My Design Academia's NID DAT preparation in Pune is structured to build both creative and analytical skills, ideal for students starting NID entrance coaching after 12th. Key preparation tips:

  • Practise sketching and visual communication every day
  • Solve previous NID DAT papers to understand patterns
  • Build a portfolio with original, process-driven work
  • Train under NID alumni mentors for real exam-level studio readiness
  • Develop design awareness: study materials, forms & function

What Makes M.Des Prep Different?

Most candidates underestimate how different the NID DAT M.Des is from undergraduate design exams. It goes beyond sketching. You are assessed on domain depth, design research, and your ability to think like a practitioner. Our NID M.Des entrance coaching in Pune is built specifically for this: discipline-focused sessions, portfolio critique, and mock studio tests led by working designers. A few things that matter most:

  • Strengthen your discipline knowledge: theory, history, and application
  • Practise both objective reasoning and subjective design responses
  • Build a research-driven portfolio suited to your chosen M.Des discipline
  • Prepare for studio tests with hands-on ideation and concept presentation
