What is JEE Main Paper 2A?

JEE Main Paper 2A is the gateway to B.Arch programmes at India's most prestigious institutions: SPAs, NITs, IITs, and more. A 400-mark exam split across Mathematics, Aptitude, and a hands-on Drawing Test, it rewards students who can think analytically and express ideas visually. If architecture is the goal, this is the exam that opens those doors. What the Exam Actually Demands

Paper 2A is unlike any other entrance exam. It combines hard mathematics with spatial reasoning and freehand drawing, all in a single sitting. The Drawing Test alone is pen and paper, offline, and tests your ability to observe, compose, and render under pressure. Most students who struggle with it have never had structured drawing training. Scheme of Examination & Marking Scheme

JEE Main Paper 2A (B.Arch) consists of three parts totalling 400 marks:

Part Subject Type of Questions Mode of Exam Marks
Part I Mathematics MCQs + Numerical Value Type Questions Computer-Based (CBT) 100
Part II Aptitude Test Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) Computer-Based (CBT) 200
Part III Drawing Test Drawing-based subjective questions Pen & Paper (Offline) 100
Total — — — 400
