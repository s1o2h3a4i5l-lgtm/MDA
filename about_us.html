Our Founders

WHO WE ARE

My Design Academia is a best design coaching institute in Viman Nagar, Pune founded by NIDians, IITians, and alumni from top architectural colleges who have been actively working in the design field for nearly a decade, earning recognition through multiple international accolades.

What truly sets us apart from conventional coaching institutes is this: the people who founded My Design Academia are the same people who walk into the classroom every day. Our founders bring the weight of real-world experience directly into your learning journey — from live project exposure and design thinking methodologies to the insider knowledge of what top institutions are really looking for.

With 5–10 years of combined teaching and industry experience, they have consistently mentored students who have gone on to secure admissions in top institutions like NID, IIT, NIFT and top architecture colleges.

The idea behind starting My Design Academia was simple — to create a space where aspiring designers receive the right mentorship, creative training, and guidance needed to enter top design colleges and build successful design careers.

Our Story

WHY WE STARTED THIS

While interacting with students and observing the coaching landscape, we realized that many design entrance institutes focus only on exam preparation and theoretical training.

However, design education requires much more than that — it requires creativity, observation, sketching skills, and strong design thinking.

My Design Academia was started with the aim of providing students with complete design mentorship, where they not only prepare for entrance exams but also develop the creative skills needed for a successful career in design and architecture.

Our goal is to create a learning environment where students receive design career guidance, feel confident, motivated, and inspired to explore their creative potential.
