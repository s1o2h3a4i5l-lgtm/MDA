The Programme

What is NIFT B.Des?

NIFT is not just a college — it is where India's fashion industry finds its next generation of talent. The B.Des programme is 4 years of rigorous, hands-on training in disciplines like Fashion Design, Textile Design, Leather Design, Accessory Design, and Knitwear Design. Every semester pushes students to think visually, work with materials, and solve real design problems. Graduates walk into careers at labels, studios, and brands that most people only dream of working for. The entrance is competitive — but with NIFT B.Des coaching in Viman Nagar, Pune at My Design Academia, your preparation is in the right hands.

How to Crack NIFT Entrance?

Most students underestimate the NIFT entrance until they sit for it. GAT tests your aptitude. CAT tests your creative thinking and illustration ability — and carries 50% of your total score. The Situation Test then asks you to build something from scratch, with your hands, under time pressure. NIFT B.Des preparation after 12th done right means training for all three — not just the written paper. At My Design Academia, we build each student's creative confidence from the ground up, with structured mock tests, personal feedback, and studio-based practice that makes the real exam feel familiar.

  • Practise illustration and creative thinking every day
  • Solve previous NIFT CAT papers to understand patterns
  • Build hands-on model-making skills for the Situation Test
  • Train your GAT with structured aptitude practice
  • Develop design awareness — materials, colour & composition

The Programme

What is NIFT M.Des?

Your undergraduate degree got you here. The NIFT M.Des takes you further. It is a 2-year postgraduate programme by the National Institute of Fashion Technology — one of India's most respected institutions — that turns design graduates into specialists. Open to graduates from any discipline, M.Des trains students in advanced design thinking, research, and creative leadership across fashion, textile, accessory, and related fields. The entrance evaluates GAT aptitude, CAT creative ability, and a Personal Interview — each demanding a different kind of readiness. At My Design Academia, our NIFT M.Des preparation classes in Viman Nagar, Pune are built to cover all three — with the depth this level of entrance actually demands.

The PI Makes All the Difference

Most candidates preparing for NIFT M.Des underestimate the Personal Interview. It carries 30% of your total score — the same as the CAT — and it is where examiners decide whether you think like a designer or just trained like one. Add to that a GAT that expects sharper analytical and case-study skills than the UG entrance, and it becomes clear why NIFT postgraduate design entrance coaching needs to be fundamentally different from standard exam prep.

  • We work on your design vocabulary and portfolio articulation
  • Interview confidence built through structured mock sessions
  • CAT creative training at postgraduate depth and complexity
  • GAT with sharper case-study and analytical focus
  • Getting shortlisted is only half the battle — we prepare you for all of it
