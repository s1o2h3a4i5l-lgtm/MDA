What You Get

Foreign Portfolio
Programme

The Foreign Portfolio Programme is a comprehensive coaching course designed for aspiring design students who wish to pursue bachelor's or master's degrees at some of the world's most prestigious design universities. Through personalised, one-on-one mentorship, students are guided through the entire portfolio-building process — from ideating and developing projects to curating, presenting, and articulating their work to international admissions committees. The programme is tailored to meet the specific requirements of each discipline and institution, ensuring every student submits a portfolio that is not only visually compelling but strategically positioned for their target schools.

What the Programme Covers
Project IdeationDeveloping original projects that reflect your design thinking and creative identity
Portfolio CurationSelecting, sequencing, and presenting work for maximum impact with admissions committees
Design NarrativeBuilding a coherent, compelling story across your portfolio that answers — who are you as a designer?
University ResearchMapping your profile to the right institutions and tailoring your submission to their specific expectations
Statement of PurposeGuidance on articulating your background, intent, and design vision in written applications
