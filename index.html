My Design Academia is Pune's the best design entrance coaching institute in Viman Nagar, Pune guiding students into India's top design and architecture colleges: NID, IIT, NIFT and top Architecture colleges

Choosing a career in design can be confusing for both students and parents. We offer free design career counselling to help you find the right direction and make informed decisions. The session also covers top design colleges, their entrance exams, and exam patterns.

Our training program covers all key areas of design entrance preparation, including design aptitude, sketching, and creative thinking, helping students build the skills and confidence needed to succeed in top design institutes. Along with exam preparation, we also provide portfolio preparation for design colleges and personalized guidance to help students shape a successful design career.

Our faculty are alumni who have personally cracked these exams and gone on to build real careers in the design industry. They don't just teach, they mentor, guide, and prepare your child for everything these exams demand.
